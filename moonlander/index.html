    OBJECTIVES

      Land soft · walk the moon · fly again

      HOW TO PLAY

      Touch down gently on a flat pad, climb out, scavenge, then blast off for the next one. Each successful landing makes the next one a little harder.

      2D LANDER

      • W / ↑ / Space thrust (burns fuel)
      • A D / ← → rotate
      • M inventory · Esc pause / settings
      • On touch: on-screen ◀ ▲ ▶ buttons

      FUEL, BRIEFLY

      • Refuel on the moon.

      LANDING CHECKLIST

      • Pick a flat pad — bonus pads marked X2 / X3 / X5 multiply your score.

      DON'T

      • Come in hot — velocity is the #1 killer.
      • Land on a slope — "CRASHED ON UNEVEN TERRAIN".
      • Burn all your fuel far from a pad — you'll glide into the ground.

      HIGH SCORES

        WELCOME, COMMANDER

        3D WALK

        • W A S D / ↑ ← ↓ → walk + strafe
        • drag mouse / trackpad look around (release to stop)
        • E / Space interact · pick up · board lander
        • Q / G drop carried item
        • M map · C reset camera · Esc pause / settings
        • On touch: left joystick to move, drag the right side of the screen to look, tap items or the lander to interact.

        ON THE MOON

        • FUEL DRUM tops up your tanks.
        • SUPPLY CRATE adds a repair kit.
        • SAMPLE +50 score, banks toward SAMPLE COLLECTOR.
        • DAMAGED PROBE spends a kit, +500 score.
        • Walk to your lander and tap it (or press E) when you're done.
        • Tap a CARRY entry on the HUD to drop that item where you're standing.

        ANYWHERE

        • Esc settings (volume, invert-Y, fullscreen)
        • 🔇 / 🔊 mute toggle, top-right corner

        WELCOME TO THE LANDER

        2D LANDING

        • W / ↑ / Space thrust
        • A D / ← → rotate
        • Touch a flat pad with both feet at low speed and near-vertical angle.
        • On touch: hold the on-screen ◀ ▲ ▶ buttons. They support held + simultaneous press (thrust + rotate together).

        WATCH THE GAUGES

        • FUEL — runs out, run ends. Stow drums on the moon to refill.
        • HULL — crashes shave HP. Stow repair parts to restore.
        • HEALTH — the astronaut's suit. Heal at habitats.

        BONUS PADS

        • X2 X3 X5 multiply the landing score.
        • Beginner pads (no multiplier) drop a fuel drum on the moon.
